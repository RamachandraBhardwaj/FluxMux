- **Throughput:** 1K-100K msg/sec (depends on batch size)
- **Memory:** O(batch size)

### Kafka Inspector
- **Latency:** 100-1000ms (depends on partition count)
- **Throughput:** Limited by Kafka fetch rate
- **Memory:** O(message count)

### Pipe Transformations
- **Latency:** 10-100ms (depends on operations)
- **Throughput:** 10K-1M msg/sec
- **Memory:** O(message size)
